Band created in 2023 after meeting the prolific musicians of the renowned organization DENIZEN RECORDS. Began so I could embarrass myself. All albums recorded on tape. Watch us live on YouTube.

DAS BOOTY (2023)

Recorded with Bradford Barker III,
in his apartment in Cambridge, MA,
on Tascam Portastudio 424 MKi.
Mostly acoustic album.
Scotty Malcolm’s debut on the historical online landmark Denizen Records.

Includes hit tracks:
“MarioKart Tournament”
“If I Wuz Sexy”
“There are Girls I Want To Have Sex With”

Bandcamp exclusive.
1 tape replication exists.

BALLIN (2023)

Follow-up to “Das Booty.”
Recorded alone,
except Alice Cohen on drums (“Like A Fool”)
and a guest vocal by my ex.
It was an okay album; I feel alright about it.

I can picture this time clearly:
three days in Cape Cod at summer’s start
with my ex and her family,
then back home to make this record.

Bandcamp exclusive.
1 tape replication exists.

EMMA TREMONT THE 1ST (2023)

Recorded that same summer after “Ballin,”
dedicated to my ex-lover.
I think this one’s pretty good.

Bandcamp exclusive.

HEART OF A WOLF, SOUL OF A DRAGON (2024)

First ninja album with a full band.
Recorded in the basement of Pasta Planet
on Gavin Caine’s 8‑track reel‑to‑reel.
Lineup: Scotty (guitar/vocals), Colin Ratcliff (guitar),
Aidan Shapero (drums), Jacob Lopez (bass).
“Gooner Heaven” by Dew Myron & Gavin Caine.

“4.5/5” – RateYourMusic.com

“One of the funniest records ever made: sophomoric, crude, onanist, and it rocks!”
– sigfried

Released worldwide on Spotify, iTunes, Bandcamp.
