Scotty Malcolm is Scotty Malcolm’s personal name and is one of his many musical projects.

ARTHUR FLOWERS: ELF ROCK (2021)

Scotty’s debut album. Created in his hippie era. Some of the songs were written alongside Melody Trautner.

“4.0/5” - RateYourMusic.com

Included in a list entitled “101 DIY Albums you must hear before you die.” ‘Juder’ says, “Scotty is a visionary… indie pop to heal the soul and clear the mind… fantasy and reality… intertwining. Listen while munching on leaves.”

A music video was made for the single “Mirror Man.” Watch the video. I was 19 then; I’m now 23. Nino Aiyer played drums on most songs. Eoin played bass on 1–2 tracks. Mogi Taylor played sax. Mixed and mastered by Justin Kao.

THE SECRET COLOR (2021)

Computer album made in Nashville with Rick in about two weeks. Really fun to make.

The first time I drank coffee was during this session—I fell in love with it. Years later it makes me jittery, so now I drink tea instead.

Cover art by Nico Hagg. Mixed and mastered by Justin Kao.

DOUBLE ALBUM EXTRAVAGANZA (2023)

Compilation cassette on HappenTwice combining Elf Rock and A Crazy: Named Man. Limited release (~25–50 copies).

RAT BASTARD (2023)

Released while I was in college. Concept based on my alter ego “Rat Bastard” who sabotages my life. Didn’t promote much—still okay.

THE PLANET OF THE GREAT SCOTTY (2024)

Compilation of 2024 songs on cassette and YouTube. Recorded on Logic, Ableton, and 4‑track cassette.

CORN: TO THE FIRE AND FLAMES (2024)

Recorded end of December on 4‑track cassette after moving to Brooklyn. Breakup album—maybe my best yet. Emo, regrets, mistakes. Released on Bandcamp via Denizen Records.
